# Sprite Credits

All art in this directory is derived from the [Tuxemon](https://github.com/Tuxemon/Tuxemon)
project, an open-source monster-catching RPG. Tuxemon's art assets are licensed
under [CC-BY-SA 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/) (with some
pieces under GPL-3.0); the derived tiles and sprites here are redistributed under
the same CC-BY-SA terms.

No Nintendo / Game Freak / Pokémon assets are used anywhere on this site.

## Files

- `tile_grass.png`, `tile_path.png`, `tile_flowers.png`, `tile_tree.png`,
  `tile_fence.png` — cropped/composited from
  `mods/tuxemon/gfx/tilesets/core_outdoor.png`
- `tile_tallgrass.png` — `core_outdoor.png` grass base composited with
  `mods/tuxemon/animations/tileset/grass.png` overlay frames
- `tile_sign.png` — `core_outdoor.png` grass base composited with
  `mods/tuxemon/gfx/tilesets/signs.png`
- `house.png` — cropped from `mods/tuxemon/gfx/tilesets/core_buildings.png`
  (Tilemap 0 cottage)
- `adventurer.png`, `postboy.png`, `professor.png`, `knight.png`,
  `scientist.png`, `cooldude.png`, `monk.png` — overworld character sheets
  from `mods/tuxemon/sprites/` (3 walk frames × 4 directions, 16×32 per frame)
- `vivaan_battle.png` — front-facing trainer cropped from
  `mods/tuxemon/gfx/sprites/player/adventurer.png`

Individual Tuxemon artists are credited in the Tuxemon repository's
[CREDITS file](https://github.com/Tuxemon/Tuxemon/blob/development/CREDITS.md);
notable tileset/sprite authors include Buch, Kelvin Shadewing, George Bailey,
ArMM1998, Isaiah658, and the Tuxemon team.
